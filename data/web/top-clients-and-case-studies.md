## Client concentration note
As an enterprise services firm, Cognizant's revenue is diversified across thousands of clients, but a meaningful share comes from large, long-tenured relationships in Health Sciences and Financial Services (its two largest segments). Most contracts are terminable on short notice, which the company flags as a risk factor in its 10-K.
