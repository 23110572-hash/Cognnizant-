# Culture and Values

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/about-cognizant/culture-and-values

## Purpose
We engineer modern businesses to improve everyday life.

## Vision
Every choice we make aligns to our vision: to become the preeminent technology services provider to the Global 2000 C-Suite.

## Values
Cognizant's values form the bedrock of its culture and define how the company works together to serve stakeholders:
- Work as one: think beyond roles, relying on each other's strengths to win as a team.
- Raise the bar: always aim for excellence in how we work and what we deliver.
- Dare to innovate: push boundaries and take chances to reimagine what's possible.
- Do the right thing: lead with integrity and always make the ethical choice.
- Own it: own the outcomes for the company, colleagues, and community.

## Employee affinity groups
Associate-led communities built around shared interests, backgrounds, and experiences, open to all associates:
- Black, Latin and Indigenous Group (BLING)
- Veterans Network
- Embrace LGBTQ+
- Race Equality Network (REN) — across EMEA, APJ, and LATAM
- Pan-Asian Group
- Unite — associates with disabilities, caregivers, and allies
- Women Empowered (WE)
- Working Families
