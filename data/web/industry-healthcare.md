# Healthcare (Industry Solutions)

## Overview
"Unifying healthcare for an AI-agent-ready future." Cognizant helps payers and providers operate more efficiently with digital solutions that reduce administrative friction, support better outcomes, and keep clinical decisions and oversight in human hands.

## Scale (as published)

## Segments served
- Payer solutions: connect payers and providers for a frictionless member experience with agent-ready intelligence.
- Provider solutions: technology and expertise for seamless patient experiences with agent-ready workflows.

## TriZetto Unify
A platform strategy spanning payer and provider workflows. A new headless API model treats AI agents as first-tier consumers of TriZetto Unify. Electronic Prior Authorization is the first solution to go live. Designed to support faster coordination across payer-provider workflows while keeping clinical decisions and oversight in human hands.
