# Cognizant — Executive Bios and Leadership Thinking

Sources: Official Cognizant leadership bio pages and earnings-call quotes. These capture each leader's background and stated focus (their public "thinking"). Personal social-media/LinkedIn content is intentionally not scraped; the views below come from official, public company sources.

## Ravi Kumar S — Chief Executive Officer
- Appointed CEO in January 2023. Sets strategic direction, promotes a "client-first" culture, focuses on sustainable growth and long-term shareholder value.
- Named to the TIME AI 100 (2025) as an influential leader in AI.
- Prior: President of Infosys (2016–2022), led the global services organization and chaired Infosys BPM. Earlier roles at PwC, Cambridge Technology Partners, Oracle, and Sapient. Began his career as a nuclear scientist at the Bhabha Atomic Research Center (India).
- External roles: board of TransUnion; board of governors, New York Academy of Sciences; Chair of the AI working committee and board member of the US Chamber of Commerce; board of the US-India Strategic Partnership Forum (USISPF).
- Education: MBA, Xavier Institute of Management (India).
- Stated thinking: champions the "AI builder" strategy; frames AI as augmenting people and tasks (not just replacing roles); emphasizes scaling AI across the enterprise and returning Cognizant to the "winner's circle."

## Jatin Dalal — Chief Financial Officer
- Joined Cognizant December 2023. Oversees worldwide FP&A, accounting/controllership, tax, treasury, internal audit, corporate development, investor relations, and enterprise risk.
- Prior: President and CFO of Wipro (CFO 2015–2019; earlier CFO of Wipro's IT Business 2011–2015); led Wipro Ventures ($300M strategic investment arm). Began career at General Electric (1999), joined Wipro 2002.
- Credentials: Chartered Accountant, Cost Accountant, Chartered Management Accountant, CFA; Wharton Advanced Management Program; Stanford Advanced Computer Security certificate; engineering degree (NIT Surat) and finance/international business diploma (NMIMS Mumbai).
- Stated thinking: emphasizes operational rigor, >100% free-cash-flow conversion, margin expansion, and disciplined capital return.

## Babak Hodjat — Chief AI Officer
- Leads AI Research Labs; advances state-of-the-art AI, builds differentiated AI features into offerings, and leads AI-for-good initiatives.
- Prior: cofounder and CEO of Sentient (core tech behind a very large distributed AI system) and founder of the first AI-driven hedge fund (Sentient Investment Management). Senior director of engineering at Sybase iAnywhere; cofounder/CTO of Dejima — primary inventor of the agent-oriented technology behind Apple's Siri.
- Credentials: 50+ papers and 39 issued US patents; expert in NLP, machine learning, evolutionary algorithms, distributed AI. Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence, Kyushu University (Japan).
- Stated thinking: deep advocate of agentic and evolutionary AI; pushes responsible, research-grounded AI.

## Surya Gummadi — President, Americas
- Responsible for strategy and operations across the US, Latin America, and Canada, plus the global large-deals team.
- 20+ years at Cognizant; previously SVP of Global Health Sciences (led 80,000 professionals). Led the TriZetto acquisition (the company's largest) and its integration.
- Education: mechanical engineering, IIT Bombay; grew up in Hyderabad, moved to the US in 2003.
- Stated thinking: client-centric, growth-oriented team builder; strong in healthcare/health sciences transformation.

## Manoj Mehta — President, EMEA
- Responsible for strategy and operations across Northern, Central, and Southern Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and the UK & Ireland.
- Joined Cognizant in 2005; led the global large-deals team; former Global Head of Industry 4.0 and Industrial IoT; interim Head of Global Growth Markets; commercial leadership in Continental Europe and Benelux. Prior: Conclusion NV (Netherlands) and HCL.
- Education: BS in computer science, University of Pune.

## Ganesh Ayyar — President, Asia Pacific & Japan and Industry Solutions Group
- Leads APJ (Australia, New Zealand, ASEAN, Greater China, India, Japan) and the global Industry Solutions Group (hyperspecialized, industry-led AI/digital solutions).
- Previously President of Intuitive Operations and Automation (IOA), scaling it into one of Cognizant's fastest-growing service lines. Former CEO of Mphasis (2009–2017; CEO of the Year 2016); nearly two decades at Hewlett-Packard.
- Stated thinking: emphasizes culture, client impact, and purpose-led leadership; frequent speaker on the "AI-powered enterprise" and reframing the AI shift as an opportunity to elevate people (continuous learning embedded in daily work).
