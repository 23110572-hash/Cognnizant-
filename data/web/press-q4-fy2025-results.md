# Cognizant Reports Fourth Quarter and Full-Year 2025 Results

Source: https://news.cognizant.com/2026-02-04-Cognizant-Reports-Fourth-Quarter-and-Full-Year-2025-Results
Date: February 4, 2026 (Teaneck, N.J.)

## Headline highlights
- Q4 2025 revenue of $5.3 billion, up 4.9% year-over-year (3.8% in constant currency).
- Full-year 2025 revenue of $21.1 billion, up 7.0% year-over-year (6.4% in constant currency).
- Full-year operating margin of 16.1%, up 140 bps year-over-year; Adjusted Operating Margin of 15.8%, up 50 bps.
- Full-year GAAP EPS of $4.56 (up 1%); Adjusted EPS of $5.28 (up 11%).
- Trailing 12-month bookings of $28.4 billion, up 5% year-over-year (book-to-bill ~1.3x); 28 large deals signed in 2025 (12 in Q4).
- In 2026, expects to return $1.6 billion to shareholders via buybacks and dividends, including $1 billion of repurchases.
- Cash dividend increased 6.5% to $0.33 per share for Q1 2026.
- 2026 constant currency revenue growth guidance: 4.0% to 6.5%.
- 2026 Adjusted Operating Margin guidance: 15.9% to 16.1% (10 to 30 bps expansion).

CEO Ravi Kumar S noted Cognizant has over 350,000 employees and that 2025 was a defining year as it put its "AI builder strategy" in motion and returned to the "winner's circle" two years ahead of target, with large-deal TCV growth of nearly 50% year-over-year. CFO Jatin Dalal cited free cash flow conversion above 100% of net income and $2 billion returned to shareholders.

## Employee metrics
- Voluntary Attrition (Tech Services), TTM: 13.9% (period ended Dec 31, 2025), vs 14.5% (Sep 30, 2025) and 15.9% (Dec 31, 2024).
- Total headcount as of Dec 31, 2025: 351,600 (up 1,800 from Sep 30, 2025 and 14,800 from Dec 31, 2024).
