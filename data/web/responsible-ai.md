# Responsible AI

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/about-cognizant/responsible-ai

## Overview
Cognizant is committed to promoting ethical, safe, and trustworthy AI aligned with its values and global standards. Through Cognizant Trust (its responsible AI / RAI framework), it works to ensure AI systems are secure, reliable, and scalable.

Three strategic drivers:
- AI regulation and compliance: governance to stay ahead of evolving global AI laws and reduce compliance risk.
- Market trust and performance: transparent, auditable systems aligned with performance metrics.
- Business growth and opportunity: scaling responsible AI in high-demand markets.

## Responsible AI services
- RAI readiness and governance services: strategy, governance, compliance assessments, roadmaps, operating models aligned to ISO 42001 and NIST AI RMF.
- RAI implementation services: deploy controls, tools, and frameworks; system integration, automation, enterprise-wide monitoring.
- Monitoring and operational support: continuous monitoring, performance oversight, recourse pathways, incident response playbooks.
- Lifecycle assurance and testing: testing for bias, performance, traceability, and responsible system decommissioning.
- Audit and regulatory alignment: audit readiness, documentation, frameworks conforming to evolving AI laws.
- Trustworthy and sustainable AI strategy: responsible growth roadmaps embedding trust into every stage of AI adoption.

## Global standards alignment
Cognizant aligns with: OECD AI principles, UNESCO ethical AI guidelines, EU AI Act, NIST AI Risk Management Framework, and ISO AI standards.

## Responsible AI standards (grounded in the Code of Ethics)
- Safe and reliable
- Transparent and explainable
- Human focused
- Secure and privacy-enhanced
- Fair and inclusive
- Accountable and auditable
- Scalable and sustainable

These are operationalized through the Cognizant Trust framework across the full AI lifecycle.

## Partnerships and research
- Strategic Partner of the World Economic Forum, contributing to AI policy and governance efforts.
- Cognizant AI Lab advances the science behind responsible AI (safe, transparent, reliable systems).
