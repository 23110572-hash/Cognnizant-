# Consulting

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/services/consulting

## Overview
Cognizant Consulting delivers enterprise value from strategy to delivery. Consulting teams combine forward-thinking strategy, deep domain expertise, and AI-driven insights to deliver measurable outcomes.

Working side-by-side with client teams, Cognizant co-creates intelligent and adaptable roadmaps that guide the enterprise from today's realities to tomorrow's possibilities, moving from strategy to results and unlocking the full potential of AI.

## BASIS framework
BASIS is Cognizant's modular framework for turning agentic ambition into durable, enterprise-grade value, anchored in context, governance, and operational redesign (enterprise process redesign).

## Approach: putting AI to work together
- Winning with AI requires a human-first approach.
- Advisors partner with clients to develop AI solutions anchored in business goals, context, and real-world use cases.
- Cognizant brings strategy, technology, and workforce together to help clients move from AI-ready to AI-optimized, embedding solutions into how people work, make decisions, and create value.
