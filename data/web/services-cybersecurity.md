# Cybersecurity Services

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/services/cybersecurity-services

## Overview
"Cyber resilience. Delivered." AI is changing the security equation, accelerating threats and expanding risk across identities, cloud, data, and AI systems. Cognizant helps strengthen cyber defense and secure AI so clients can scale autonomy with trust.

Key challenges in the new security era: AI-accelerated threats, tool sprawl and alert noise, expanded attack surface, and proof-driven compliance.

## Dual mandate
- AI-driven defense: use AI to improve detection, triage, and response, reducing dwell time and operational drag.
- Security for AI: secure AI models, pipelines, and agents so innovation scales with integrity.

## Cognizant Managed Cybersecurity
An AI-orchestrated, managed cyber defense service powered by Neuro Cybersecurity. It unifies detection, response, and risk across the digital estate through a single intelligent security fabric, integrating threat intelligence, workload security, vulnerability management, and risk visibility.

Key benefits:
- Unified, AI-orchestrated defense across cloud, network, endpoints, and workloads
- Proactive detection and coordinated response
- Predictable cost with flexible pricing and no CapEx
- 24/7 managed operations with outcome-driven visibility
