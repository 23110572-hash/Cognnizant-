Sources: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/about-cognizant/partners and service/industry pages.

## Overview
Cognizant works with the most trusted names in technology to bring best-in-class capabilities to clients. It organizes partners into Strategic Partners (large-scale, deep co-innovation) and an Extended Partner Network (targeted, specialized capabilities). It maintains 85+ strategic alliances.
