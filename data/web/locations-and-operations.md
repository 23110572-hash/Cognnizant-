# Cognizant — Locations and Operations

Sources: Cognizant FY2025 results; Wikipedia (paraphrased); company announcements.

## Headquarters
- Global headquarters: Teaneck, New Jersey, USA (Bergen County), at 300 Frank W. Burr Blvd.
- Originally founded and still operating a major delivery base in Chennai, India.

## Delivery model and geographic mix (FY2025 revenue)
- North America: ~74.8% of revenue (~$15.78B)
- Europe: ~19.0% (~$4.01B) — United Kingdom ~$1.92B, Continental Europe ~$2.09B
- Rest of World: ~6.2% (~$1.32B)

## India operations (largest workforce base)
- A large majority of employees are based in India across 13+ cities.
- Largest centers: Hyderabad and Chennai (each 50,000+ employees), followed by Bengaluru (~40,000). Hyderabad became Cognizant's largest single center globally in 2024.
- New 8,000-seat campus under construction in Visakhapatnam (phased through 2033; first phase ~3,000 associates by early 2029).
- New AI Lab and Cognizant Moment Studio, plus a Next-Gen Cyber Defense Center, opened in Bengaluru.
- A delivery center also opened in Bhubaneswar.

## Global delivery centers
Local, regional, and global delivery centers operate in (among others): the UK, Australia, Hungary, Netherlands, Spain, China, Philippines, Canada, Brazil, Argentina, Mexico, and Costa Rica.

## Innovation footprint
- AI Lab in San Francisco (extended by the India AI Lab in Bengaluru).
- Cognizant Moment studios (digital experience practice).
- Health Sciences Innovation Center.

## Operating segments (business units)
Cognizant reports four industry segments:
- Health Sciences (~30% of revenue)
- Financial Services (~29%)
- Products and Resources (~25%)
- Communications, Media and Technology (~16%)

It is organized into vertical (industry) units and horizontal (technology/process) units, with a cross-cutting Cognizant Consulting team.
