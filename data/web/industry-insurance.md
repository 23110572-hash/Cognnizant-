# Insurance (Industry Solutions)

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/industries/insurance-digital-transformation

## Overview
"Next-gen insurance technology solutions: creating exponential advantage." Cognizant helps insurers improve efficiency and growth with industry-specific solutions powered by AI, data, and automation, modernizing legacy technology and processes to meet customer demand for digital, personalized experiences while reducing risk.

Key outcomes:
- Speed products to market by streamlining development and accelerating underwriting, billing, and core processes.
- Reduce operating costs by enhancing data accuracy and streamlining manual tasks.
- Achieve efficiencies by automating workflows and integrating AI and generative AI.

## Segments served
- Property & casualty (P&C): automate front- and back-office processes, optimize partner implementations, deliver personalized digital customer experiences.
- Life & annuity, group & retirement: accelerate underwriting, increase efficiency and responsiveness, reduce complexity.

## Offerings
- Insurance innovation as a service
- Insurance platform modernization
- AI for insurance (including generative AI)

## Selected case studies
- Talcott: cloud-based modernization, reduced capex/opex by 25% and cut costs by 20%.
- US carrier test automation: scriptless solution accelerates QA and saves $1.6M.
- P&C provider API-driven B2B: speeds quote-to-buy with real-time integration.
- British insurer RPA solution: automates 70+ processes, saving £7.5M annually.
