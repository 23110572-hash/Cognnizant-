# Cognizant — Talent, HR Policies and Careers

Sources: Cognizant Careers (careers.cognizant.com), Talent Worldwide, Culture & Values, and ESG pages.

## Employer profile
- Great Place to Work certified.
- Recognized by Fortune (One of America's Most Innovative Companies; One of the World's Best Employers), The Wall Street Journal (250 Best Managed Companies), and TIME (World's Best Companies).
- Included in Forbes' America's Best Employers for Veterans 2025.
- Tagline: empowering people to "combine your passions with our reach to engineer a smarter, more connected world."

## Workforce scale
- ~350,000+ employees globally (headcount ~351,600 as of Dec 31, 2025).
- Voluntary attrition (Tech Services, TTM) of 13.9% at year-end 2025, down from 15.9% a year earlier.

## Career paths / talent segments
- Students and new grads (e.g., Graduate Consultant program).
- Experienced professionals.
- Alumni network.
- A "talent community" lets candidates receive tailored job, event, and company updates.

## Learning & development
- Strong emphasis on employee-driven career growth ("be in the driver's seat of your journey") with dedicated L&D programs.
- Synapse skilling initiative doubles as an internal learning program and external philanthropy: goal to skill two million people by 2030 (original one-million-by-2026 goal outpaced; 400,000+ participants reported by mid-2025).
- Continuous learning embedded into daily workflows as part of the AI-era talent strategy.

## Inclusion and affinity groups
Associate-led communities open to all: BLING (Black, Latin and Indigenous Group), Veterans Network, Embrace LGBTQ+, Race Equality Network (REN), Pan-Asian Group, Unite (disabilities/caregivers), Women Empowered (WE), Working Families.

## Benefits and culture signals
- Benefits highlighted by associates include medical/family support (e.g., caregiver support, maternity leave reintegration).
- Global citizenship/volunteering: 47,000+ unique volunteers contributed 220,000+ hours.

## Talent search / recruiting notes
- Hiring is managed via the global careers portal (careers.cognizant.com) and a Talent Community signup.
- Cognizant historically is among the largest recruiters of engineers and MBAs in the IT services industry.
- Candidate and Talent Search Privacy Notices govern recruiting data; the company publishes a Fraud Alert warning candidates about recruitment scams and a Notice to Third Party Recruiters.
- Largest talent hubs are in India (Chennai, Hyderabad, Bengaluru), with significant US delivery hiring.
