# Intelligent Process Automation (IPA)

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/services/intelligent-process-automation

## Overview
"Enhance process automation with the power of AI." Cognizant connects processes, people, and insights across the enterprise with AI-enabled intelligent process automation (IPA) to achieve breakthrough performance at lower costs, combining business and process expertise with AI-enabled digital platforms.

## Spotlight platform
- Cognizant Neuro Business Processes: AI-powered automation platform that accelerates data integration and reporting, boosts efficiency, and transforms processes.

## Offerings
- Cognizant advisory services
- Cognizant solutions
- Cognizant implementation services
- Cognizant managed services

The practice combines advisory services with vendor partnerships (e.g., UiPath, Automation Anywhere, Microsoft Power Automate) and integrated solutions to create and execute strategic automation roadmaps. There is a clear theme of evolving from traditional RPA to agentic automation at scale.
