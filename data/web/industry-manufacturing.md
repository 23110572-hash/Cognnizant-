# Manufacturing (Industry Solutions)

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/industries/manufacturing-technology-solutions

## Overview
"Digital Manufacturing Solutions: making the transition to digital manufacturing." Through analytics, automation, AI, smart factories, and integrated systems, Cognizant's digital manufacturing solutions help manufacturers make business decisions based on real-time contextual data.

## Consulting & solutions
- Field services management for industrial manufacturers
- Manufacturing the modern enterprise
- REACH compliance framework for chemical manufacturers

## Segments served
- Industrial manufacturing: heavy equipment, HVAC/R, industrial supplies, automation systems; build connected products.
- Process manufacturing: chemicals, building materials, pulp and paper, mining; streamline processes and gain operational visibility.

## Selected case studies
- Orica: IT modernization with new SAP S/4HANA Digital Core (saves $2M); higher manufacturing agility.
- Global water technology company: process redesign simplifies operations, saving $25 million.

## Thought leadership themes
Generative AI in manufacturing, industrial IoT, Industry 4.0, digital supply chain, decision process automation.
