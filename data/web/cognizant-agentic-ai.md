# Cognizant — Agentic AI

Source: Cognizant newsroom and investor relations (2025–2026)

## Focus on the agentic enterprise
Cognizant is centering its offerings on agentic AI — systems where autonomous AI
agents and humans work together across business functions to accelerate outcomes.
The company's goal is to give enterprises a composable, platform-agnostic path to
becoming "agentic enterprises," moving from proofs of concept to large-scale
production deployments.

## Cognizant Agent Foundry
Launched July 10, 2025, Cognizant Agent Foundry helps enterprises design, deploy,
and orchestrate autonomous AI agents at scale. It provides a composable,
platform-agnostic pathway so humans and AI agents can work in concert across
functions and unlock business value from AI.

## Neuro AI Multi-Agent Accelerator
A no-code multi-agent orchestration framework (part of the Neuro platform suite)
that lets organizations design, deploy, and scale networks of collaborating
agents. Cognizant open-sourced it in 2025 for research and academic use.

## Cross-platform interoperability
- June 18, 2026 — ServiceNow AI Agents now interoperate with the Neuro AI
  Multi-Agent Accelerator, giving enterprises a unified environment to orchestrate
  agents across the platforms they already run.
- June 2026 — Expanded alliance with CrowdStrike to secure the agentic enterprise.
- June 2026 — Collaboration with Snowflake to use Cortex-powered intelligent agents.

## AI governance for agents
Cognizant pairs agentic AI with governance. In June 2026 it integrated Neuro AI
Trust with ServiceNow to deliver continuous AI assurance, and it emphasizes
responsible, governed AI (Cognizant is the first IT services provider certified to
ISO/IEC 42001:2023, the AI management system standard).

## AI Lab
The Cognizant AI Lab advances decision AI and multi-agent systems. In 2025 its
research shifted toward systems that can plan, act, adapt, and collaborate in real
decision environments, producing tools such as Neuro SAN and published academic work.
