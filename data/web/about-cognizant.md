## What we do
Cognizant uses expertise proven and tested around the globe to help clients get ahead of challenges, sense opportunities sooner, and outpace change.

- Transform experiences: hyper-personalized experiences that connect faster, generate growth, and build brand loyalty.
- Reimagine processes: process automation and technology to act with insight, precision, and speed.
- Modernize technology: software built to learn and deploy processing power, unlocked in the cloud to create value in every byte of data.

The aim is to move beyond just running a business and turn it into one that sees the next best action and acts as if on intuition, in the moments that matter.

## Culture and values
Each member of the team takes responsibility for creating a culture that enables exceptional outcomes.
- What we value: help imagine, build, and implement technologies to keep clients aware and responsive.
- What we believe: a social purpose that weaves environmental and social considerations into the business model.
- Who we are: people engineer impact with clients, communities, and colleagues.
- How we behave: respect for everyone's unique voice and background; varied perspectives help the company thrive.
