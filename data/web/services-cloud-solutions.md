# Cloud Solutions

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/services/cloud-solutions

## Overview
Cognizant Cloud helps clients implement a cloud-enabled business transformation. Modern businesses gain visibility, agility, and an intuitive advantage with optimized cloud solutions, including cloud migration, cloud DevOps, cloud management, and optimization.

Cognizant brings cloud solution expertise, deep industry knowledge, and partnerships to migrate, integrate, automate, manage, and deliver outcomes that fuel innovation and growth.

## 3Cloud acquisition
Cognizant enhanced its Microsoft Azure capabilities through the acquisition of 3Cloud. Prior to acquisition, 3Cloud was Microsoft's largest Azure-dedicated partner. The combined 21,000+ Azure-certified specialists provide one of the most comprehensive Azure-focused portfolios in the industry.
