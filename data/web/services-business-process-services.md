# Business Process Services (BPS)

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/services/business-process-services

## Overview
"AI-powered business processes for the modern enterprise." Cognizant embeds context-aware intelligence into every workflow to help enterprises reimagine operations with AI and automation, blending technology, data, and human insight. BPO has evolved from a cost-saving measure into a driver of innovation, now often called Business Process Services (BPS) or Modern Business Operations.
