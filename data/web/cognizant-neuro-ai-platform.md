# Cognizant Neuro — AI Platform Suite

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/services/cognizant-platforms (and Cognizant newsroom, 2023–2026)

## Overview
Cognizant Neuro® is the company's family of AI-led platforms that sit at the
center of its "AI builder" strategy. The suite orchestrates AI, automation, and
agents across the enterprise technology stack so clients can move from isolated
AI pilots to scaled, production-grade adoption. The Neuro AI platform underpins
Cognizant's data, generative AI, and agentic AI services.

## Neuro AI
Neuro AI is Cognizant's enterprise platform for adopting generative and agentic
AI. It helps enterprises build, deploy, and orchestrate AI use cases on top of
their existing data, models, and infrastructure. Built with NVIDIA AI, the
platform delivers specialized industry large language models, reasoning agents,
and digital twins, and orchestrates across the enterprise stack.

Key components and research platforms include:
- Neuro AI Decisioning — decision-focused AI for complex business situations.
- Neuro AI System of Agent Networks (Neuro SAN) — a multi-agent orchestration
  platform from the Cognizant AI Lab for designing networks of collaborating agents.

## Neuro AI Multi-Agent Accelerator
Announced January 2025, the Neuro AI Multi-Agent Accelerator is a no-code
framework with pre-built reference agent networks that let businesses rapidly
prototype, customize, and scale multi-agent systems. It is part of a broader
Multi-Agent Services Suite for transforming business processes. In May 2025
Cognizant open-sourced the accelerator for research and academic use, and it
later won a Global AI Award for best AI product or service.

## Neuro IT Operations
Launched in 2023, Neuro IT Operations is an AI-driven automation platform that
reduces the complexity and operating cost of enterprise infrastructure and
technology, enabling an automation-first approach to IT operations.

## Neuro Cybersecurity
Introduced in October 2024, Neuro Cybersecurity is an AI-powered platform that
integrates and orchestrates point security solutions across the enterprise to
improve cyber resilience and risk management for the AI era.

## Cognizant Flowsource
Announced February 2024, Flowsource is a generative-AI-enabled platform for
modern software engineering. It connects all stages of the software development
lifecycle and brings together digital assets and tools so engineering teams can
deliver higher-quality code faster, with more control and transparency.

## Neuro AI Trust
Neuro AI Trust provides continuous AI assurance infrastructure built for
enterprise scale. In June 2026 Cognizant integrated Neuro AI Trust with
ServiceNow to support operationalized, governed AI across the enterprise.
