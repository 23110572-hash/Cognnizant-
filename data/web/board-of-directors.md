# Cognizant — Board of Directors

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/about-cognizant/corporate-governance/board-of-directors

The board is composed mostly of independent directors with backgrounds spanning global consumer, technology, healthcare, financial services, automotive, and consulting leadership.

- Stephen (Steve) J. Rohleder — Chair of Cognizant's Board; former Group Chief Executive, North America and COO of Accenture
- Ravi Kumar S — CEO of Cognizant (executive director)
- Zein Abdalla — former President of PepsiCo
- Vinita Bali — former CEO and MD of Britannia Industries; former VP of The Coca-Cola Company
- Eric Branderiz — former EVP and CFO of Enphase Energy
- Archana (Archie) Deskus — former EVP and CTO of PayPal Holdings
- John M. Dineen — former President and CEO of GE Healthcare
- Leo S. Mackay, Jr. — SVP, Ethics and Enterprise Assurance of Lockheed Martin
- Michael Patsalos-Fox — Chair of MIO Partners; former Chair of Cognizant's Board; former CEO of Stroz Friedberg; former Chair, the Americas and Senior Partner of McKinsey & Company
- Abraham (Bram) Schot — former Chairman and CEO of Audi AG
- Karima Silvent — Group Deputy General Secretary and Chief Human Resources Officer, AXA (Management Committee member)
- Joseph (Joe) M. Velli — former Senior EVP of The Bank of New York
- Sandra S. Wijnberg — former Partner, Aquiline Holdings

Note: The board composition reflects governance emphasis on independence, global industry diversity, and relevant domain expertise (technology, healthcare, financial services, HR/people, automotive/manufacturing).
