# Life Sciences (Industry Solutions)

Source: https://www.cognizant.com/us/en/industries/life-sciences-technology-solutions

## Overview
Cognizant helps life sciences companies accelerate innovation, improve patient outcomes, speed time to market, manage costs, and navigate regulatory demands through digital transformation. The company has committed a $1 billion investment in generative AI.

## Scale (as published)
- 100% of the top global pharmaceutical companies are Cognizant clients
- 9 of 10 of the top biotech companies
- 13 of 15 of the top medical device companies
- 4,850+ product experts in its partner ecosystem

## Segments served
- Medical devices: improve product development, speed to market, and patient centricity.
- Pharmaceuticals and biotech: use generative AI, intelligent digital platforms, cloud, and data to develop safer, more effective, affordable drugs.

## Products and platforms
- Cognizant Clinical Systems Connector: centralized platform for clinical trial data and records transfer.
- Cognizant Shared Investigator Platform: connects sponsors, sites, and technology providers to accelerate clinical trials.
- Health Sciences Innovation Center.

## Targeted solutions (life sciences ecosystem)
Digital health, business process services, discovery and preclinical, clinical development, regulatory compliance, pharmacovigilance, managed markets, manufacturing solutions, sales and marketing.

## Selected case study
- Global life sciences company saves $12M with IT rebuild: built a global IT estate across 90 countries in 12 months, achieving 100% SLA and infrastructure availability from day one after a spin-off (19,000 employees).
